  • What is an Automation Club?

    An Automation Club is a group of individuals who come together to explore, learn, and collaborate on automation technologies. This can include robotics, AI, IoT (Internet of Things), and other forms of automation used in industries and everyday life. The club typically provides hands-on experience, learning sessions, and project-based collaboration opportunities.

  • What types of projects do we work on in the Automation Club?

    Our members work on a wide variety of projects, including but not limited to: Building and programming robots, Designing automated systems for smart homes, Creating AI-driven solutions for real-world problems, Experimenting with industrial automation tools , Developing automation software for everyday tasks

  • Do I need any prior knowledge of automation to join?

    No prior knowledge is required to join the Automation Club. We welcome beginners and provide learning opportunities for everyone. Whether you're just starting or looking to deepen your expertise, there's something for everyone in the club.

  • When does the Automation Club meet?

    We hold regular meetings and workshops, typically once a week. The schedule varies based on the project needs, and we send out notifications to members about upcoming events. Meetings often include brainstorming sessions, guest speakers, and hands-on workshops.

  • How do I sign up for the Automation Club?

    To join the Automation Club, simply fill out the registration form on our website. After registering, you'll receive updates about upcoming meetings, projects, and events. You'll also be added to our communication channels to stay informed.
